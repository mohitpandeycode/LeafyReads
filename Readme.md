**LeafyReads** is a modern reading and publishing platform built for both **readers and writers**.  
Discover books, track your reading journey, and publish your own stories — all in one calm, beautifully designed space.

🔗 Visit: https://leafyreads.com

---

## ✨ What is LeafyReads?

LeafyReads is more than just a place to read.  
It’s a growing literary community where **readers explore stories** and **authors share their voice**.

Whether you love discovering new books or writing your own, LeafyReads gives you the tools to do both.

---

## 🌟 Features

### 📖 For Readers
- Explore a growing collection of books across genres  
- Save books and track your reading progress  
- Enjoy a clean, distraction-free reading experience  
- Access LeafyReads on any device (mobile, tablet, desktop)

### ✍️ For Authors
- Apply to become an **Author** on the platform  
- Publish your own books and written content  
- Share your stories with real readers  
- Build your presence as a writer inside the community

### 💬 Community & Feedback
- Share feedback and suggestions  
- Report issues or content concerns  
- Help shape the future of LeafyReads

### 🔒 Privacy & Security
- Your data and reading activity are handled responsibly  
- Secure user accounts and content management

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. Visit **https://leafyreads.com**
2. Create a free account
3. Browse books or start reading instantly
4. Apply to become an **Author** if you want to publish your own work
5. Read, write, and grow with the community 🌱

---

## ✍️ Become an Author

LeafyReads gives writers a space to **publish and be discovered**.

As an author, you can:
- Submit an author request
- Publish your own books or written works
- Reach readers who genuinely love stories
- Grow alongside a supportive reading community

📢 *Everyone has a story to tell — LeafyReads helps you share yours.*
