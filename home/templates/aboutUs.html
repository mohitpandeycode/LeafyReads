Our Philosophy

The principles that guide our work.

Celebrate Reading

We believe reading is fundamental. Our platform is designed to make it more accessible, joyful, and an integral part of daily life.

Community at the Core

Books are meant to be shared. We foster a welcoming and inclusive space for readers to connect over the stories they love.

Our Roadmap

From a simple idea to our vision for the future of reading.

2025 - The Spark

A Passion Project Begins

LeafyReads was born from late-night coding sessions and a shared desire for a better digital reading experience that felt both personal and connected.

2026 - The Launch

Hello, World!

We launch LeafyReads to the world with a clear focus: to provide the most beautiful and intuitive platform for discovering, tracking, and discussing books.

2027 - Building Community

Connecting Readers

Our goal is to introduce interactive book clubs, author Q&As, and collaborative reading lists to transform LeafyReads from a tool into a thriving community hub.

2028 & Beyond - The Future

Expanding Horizons

We will explore AI-powered recommendations, audiobook integration, and new tools to support independent authors, pushing the boundaries of the digital reading experience.
